Behold Beauty !

Prabhat Chakrabarty

EEEUUUAAAAHHHHHHH-AAAAAAHHHH!” “Don’t yawn like a caveman.”
Jane and Dave stood beneath the hazy blue sky. “Ah! The time and weather made me sleepy,” he looked towards her, “look at the sun rising behind the mountain."
“Damn! that’s a perfect blend of pretty and majestic,” Dave said poetically.
“Isn’t it ? I am so lucky that I get to see this with you,” Jane clutching Dave’s arm.
“Me too babe, we dreamt of this day and it’s here, we made it and what a beautiful sight!” Dave put his head over Jane’s head. The fruity smell of her shampoo intoxicating him.
“Look at those birds climbing uphill, it’s us... our journey to the top,” Jane strengthened her grip.
“I’m glad, we get to experience this view,” said Dave as he closed his eyes.
An old man, listening to this couldn’t help doing a double take. Were they seeing what he was? It was a hillock.
“Are you guys on your honeymoon?” he asked.
“Yes! Just married sir!”
“We are on our 35th year,” he said.
“Oh how romantic. Was it hard?”
“Oh, well, it’s uphill first. Then, after a few years, it’s all downhill.”
